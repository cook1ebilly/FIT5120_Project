What is Save Safe?

Save Safe's mission is to educate girls and provide them with the tools when they are in trouble. The purpose is to assist them in developing a successful plan before anything happens. From smart safety travel ideas on how to avoid date rape drugs, how to prevent rape to which way to go if you are being followed, if you are going alone to your house late at night or if you are out on your first date with someone you don't know well, and many more.

This application provides in-depth treatment of a variety of women's safety concerns. The subheadings and extensive description of these issues make it easier to follow the directions. This application's safety suggestions provide simple day-to-day precautions that can be performed to protect oneself from criminal activities.

Each of the articles with safety recommendations includes information on real-life life-threatening situations and how to prepare for them. We believe that having the information and a pre-planned set of actions for as many danger scenarios as possible will improve your chances of avoiding becoming a victim."
